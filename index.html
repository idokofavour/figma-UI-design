Judging Criteria

Key attributes

Innovation and Creativity: Evaluate the uniqueness and creativity of the solution. Consoder whether it addresses a real-world problem in a novel way or introduces innovative features.

Functionallity: Assess how well solution works. Does it perform its intended functions effectively and without major issues? Judges would consider the completeness and robustness of the solution.

Impact and Relevance: Determine the potential impact of the solution in the real world it address a significan problem, and is it relevant to the target audience? Judges would asssesss the potential social, economic, or enviromental benefits.

Technical Complexity: Evaluate the technical sophistication of the solution. Judges would Consider the complexity of the code, the use of advanced tecnologies or algorithms, and the scalability of the solution.

Adherence to Hackathon Rules: Judges will Ensure that the team adhered to the rules and guidliness of the hackathon, including deadlines, use of specific tecnologies or APIs, and any other competition-specific requirements.
